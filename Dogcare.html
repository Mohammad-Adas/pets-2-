Comprehensive Guide to Dog Care: Nutrition, Exercise, Grooming, Healthcare !"

Nutrition & Exercise

  • Feed your dog a balanced diet.

  • Provide fresh water daily.

  • Exercise your dog regularly with walks and playtime.

Grooming

  • Brush your dog's coat.

  • Bathe your dog as needed.

  • Trim your dog's nails.

  • Clean your dog's ears.

Healthcare

  • Schedule regular vet check-ups.

  • Keep vaccinations up to date.

  • Most importantly: Give your dog love and attention daily.
