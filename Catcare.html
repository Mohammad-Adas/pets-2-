Comprehensive Guide to Cat Care: Nutrition, Exercise, Grooming, Healthcare !"

Nutrition & Exercise

  • Feed your Cat a balanced diet.

  • Provide fresh water daily.

  • Creat opportunties for climbing and exploring.

Grooming

  • Brush your cat's coat regularly to reduce shedding and prevent matting.

  • Trim your cat's nails.

  • Clean your cat's ears and brush their teeth regularly.

Healthcare

  • Schedule regular vet check-ups.

  • Keep vaccinations up to date.

  • Most importantly: Give your cat love and attention daily.
